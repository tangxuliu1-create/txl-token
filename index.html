Project Overview

Tang Xuliu (TXL) is an independently developed ERC-20 token deployed on Ethereum, focused on transparent token mechanics, on-chain infrastructure experimentation, and long-term vesting research.

TXL is published as a long-term public on-chain project with transparent disclosures and verifiable infrastructure references.

TXL does not represent equity, ownership, or governance rights in any entity. All token-related activities and data are publicly verifiable on-chain.
