Tang Xuliu (TXL)

Litepaper – Experimental ERC-20 Token on Ethereum

1. Introduction

Tang Xuliu (TXL) is an experimental ERC-20 token deployed on the Ethereum blockchain. The project is created for personal exploration of on-chain assets, token mechanics, and decentralized infrastructure.

TXL is not designed as an investment product, does not represent equity, and does not promise any financial return.

2. Token Information

  • Token Name: Tang Xuliu
  • Symbol: TXL
  • Standard: ERC-20
  • Network: Ethereum Mainnet
  • Total Supply: 10,000,000 TXL
  • Supply Type: Fixed (no minting, no inflation)

3. Token Supply & Distribution

3.1 Initial Circulating Supply

1,000,000 TXL is initially available for:

  • Liquidity provisioning
  • Testing and experimentation
  • On-chain interactions

3.2 Locked Supply

9,000,000 TXL is locked using on-chain vesting contracts. Locked tokens are not transferable until released by the vesting schedule.

4. Vesting & Locking Mechanism

4.1 Token Vesting

  • Total locked amount: 9,000,000 TXL
  • Monthly release: 150,000 TXL
  • Enforced by smart contracts
  • No manual override possible

4.2 Liquidity Lock

  • Liquidity pool (LP) tokens are locked
  • Lock duration: 24 months
  • Liquidity cannot be withdrawn during the lock period
  • All locks are verifiable on-chain

5. Liquidity & Market Interaction

TXL liquidity is provided on decentralized exchanges (DEXs) on Ethereum and paired with ETH. Pricing is determined solely by market interaction.

TXL does not include buyback mechanisms, yield guarantees, revenue sharing, or price stabilization features.

6. Transparency & On-Chain Verifiability

All core components of TXL are verifiable on Ethereum mainnet, including:

  • Token contract
  • Total supply
  • Vesting contracts
  • Liquidity pool and LP lock

7. Risk Disclosure

TXL is an experimental blockchain project and involves risks such as smart contract vulnerabilities, market volatility, liquidity risk, and regulatory uncertainty.
